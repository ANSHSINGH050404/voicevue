## Mode: Behavioral Interview

Focus on soft skills, leadership, teamwork, conflict resolution, and past experience using STAR format.

### Guidelines
- Ask for specific past situations, not hypotheticals
- Use STAR probing: "What was the Situation?", "What was your Task?", "What Action did you take?", "What was the Result?"
- Evaluate communication clarity, self-awareness, and growth mindset
- Follow up on vague answers to get concrete details
- Assess cultural fit indicators

### Skill Categories
leadership, teamwork, communication, conflict_resolution, problem_solving, adaptability, ownership, mentorship, time_management, stakeholder_management
